⚖️ My Ethical Dilemma Reflection

Scenario: An IT administrator accessed employee payroll and medical records without permission. He was "just curious" about a coworker's salary.

Why this is wrong:

  • It breaks trust. Employees trust IT staff to protect their data.
  • It treats people as objects. Viewing private information without consent disrespects the person.
  • Small wrongs lead to bigger wrongs. If I peek today, I might steal tomorrow.

My commitment: I will never abuse admin privileges. If tempted, I will ask: "Would I want someone doing this to my mother's records?"

Scripture anchor: "Let your light so shine before men" (Matthew 5:16)
