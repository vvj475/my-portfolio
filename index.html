Service Learning Project

"Tech Skills for Life" Workshop

A 12-hour community service initiative to empower youth with digital literacy skills

[Photo from workshop will be added post-event]

Project Objective

Addressing technology ignorance among youth aged 12-18 in my community who prefer outdated methods for studying, communicating, and problem-solving. This 2-day workshop makes technology less scary and demonstrates how digital tools simplify schoolwork, job searching, and daily tasks.

Target Audience

Youth ages 12-18 in my local community who avoid technology due to lack of exposure or negative influences. The workshop provides hands-on training with free online tools including Google Docs, Canva, and Khan Academy.

Workshop Structure

2-Day Workshop (10 hours execution + 2 hours planning)
• Interactive sessions on technology benefits
• Hands-on training with free online tools
• Q&A panel with young adult mentors
• Post-workshop survey to measure success
