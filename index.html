About Me

I'm Victor Japhet, an IT professional based in Rwanda with a passion for cinematography, photography, and graphic design.

I believe in the power of technology to transform lives. Through my "Tech Skills for Life" workshop, I help youth overcome their fear of technology and discover new opportunities in the digital world.

My approach combines technical excellence with Christlike character - patience, integrity, and a genuine desire to serve others.
