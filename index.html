🚀 My IT Journey

My interest in technology started in an unusual place: my father's stationery store, where he had a duplicating machine and a typewriter — no computers. When digital computers first arrived in Nigeria, my organization demanded an email address. I remember going to a cybercafe and asking a waiter to help me create an email account. While other kids browsed the web, I was still a novice at typing. That moment gave me the courage to start learning. Since then, I've been passionate about computers because they make things work without stress. During my Pathway program, I learned that hurdles are steps to success — I keyed into that mindset, and it brought me here today. As an IT student, I now gain insights from around the world from home. My dream: to be part of Africa's digital transformation, making technology stress-free for everyone.
